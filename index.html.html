Introduction and Purpose

COMBINED MOVERS INC. respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information of applicants during our recruitment and hiring process. By submitting your application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide to us when you apply for a position. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Personal and Contact Details: Full name, postal address, phone number, and email address.
  • Application Information: Educational background, employment history, professional qualifications, skills, and resume/CV.
  • Identification Data: Government-issued IDs (e.g., driver's license, passport, SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG), and other supporting documents as required for employment verification.
  • Background and Reference Information: Information from professional references, former employers, or third-party background check services.
  • Sensitive Personal Information: In some cases, we may collect sensitive information like date of birth, gender, and civil status to comply with legal requirements or for statistical analysis. This information is processed with the highest level of care and security.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Recruitment and Hiring: To evaluate your qualifications and suitability for the position you applied for and to assess you for other potential roles within our company.
  • Communication: To communicate with you about your application status, schedule interviews, and provide feedback.
  • Verification: To verify your identity, credentials, and background information as part of our due diligence.
  • Legal and Regulatory Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations, such as employment laws and government reporting requirements.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information is shared only with:

  • Internal Personnel: Authorized human resources staff, hiring managers, and team members directly involved in the recruitment process.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with recruitment activities, such as background check agencies or recruitment platforms. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your information for the specific services they perform on our behalf.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for a period of up to **[insert number]** months/years after the application process is complete to consider you for future job opportunities and to comply with legal obligations. After this period, your information will be securely disposed of or anonymized to prevent identification.

If you are hired, relevant information from your application will be transferred to your official employee file and retained in accordance with our employee data privacy policy.

Data Protection and Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information. We have implemented a variety of organizational, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These measures include secure servers, access controls, and regular security audits.

Your Rights as an Applicant

Under applicable data privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct or update any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal or contractual obligations we may have to retain it.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for us to process your information at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
